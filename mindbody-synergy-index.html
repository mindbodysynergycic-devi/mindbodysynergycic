Our Founder & Our Story

A vision born from experience

"I wanted to serve individuals facing chronic respiratory diseases and mental health challenges — recognising the urgent need for a holistic, integrated approach to care."

Dr. Devi Sundar founded MindBody Synergy CIC from both personal and professional experience. Witnessing the struggles of those affected by COPD, asthma, pulmonary fibrosis, anxiety, depression, PTSD, and workplace psychological injuries, she built an organisation dedicated to holistic, community-centred care — bridging the gap between conventional healthcare and whole-person wellness.

Mission

We believe health and well-being are fundamental human rights, available to all regardless of background. Our mission is to create lasting change by supporting individuals and communities facing chronic respiratory diseases, mental health struggles, and workplace injuries — through preventative health, education, and community empowerment.

Vision

To provide holistic, community-based wellness programmes that improve physical and emotional health while promoting long-term sustainability — building a healthier, more inclusive society where individuals can manage their health and thrive.

Core Values

🌿 Holistic WellnessIntegrating physical, emotional, and mental well-being in every programme.
